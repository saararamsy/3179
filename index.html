Number of YouTube channels created by Year

The Line chart shows how YouTube channel creation has changed since its start in 2005. Initially, there's a steady rise in new channels, with a big spike in 2011. Another increase happens in 2013. However, after 2016, there's a clear drop in new channels, reaching its lowest in 2022. This could suggest that YouTube saw its biggest growth in the early 2010s, and recent years show a decline in new creators joining the platform.
